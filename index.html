Herba Mystica and Sandwich Powers

Herba Mystica

Herba Mystica is a type of rare ingredient that can be added to a sandwich while picnicing in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet. What makes these ingredients so rare is that they can only be obtained by getting them as rewards for completing 5, 6, and 7 star Tera Raid Battles.

Additionally, you are not guaranteed an Herba Mystica as a reward for completing a raid and the drop rate is pretty low. The following guide will teach you how to farm Herba Mystica most efficiently.

Sandwiches

Sandwiches are craftable items in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet that, upon consumption, grant the player "Meal Powers" to increase the likelyhood of types of encounters when roaming Paldea.

Additional Notes on Hummungo and Teensy Power:

Hummungo and Teensy Power chances are based on a set of data that dictates the minimum and maximum size of a pokémon. The data range for any wild encounter is naturally 0-255 with a random chance of spawning somewhere in this data range. A sandwich with these powers decreases the set range of finding a pokémon towards either end of the data set.

Other than the cosmetic effect of having a larger or smaller pokémon, there are also titles, or marks, that can be unlocked for getting the smallest value for a pokémon size and the largest value of a pokémon size.

Additional Notes on Sparkling Power:

Sparking power rolls can be confusing if you don't understand how shinys spawn in the overworld. The basic rates for a shiny at full odds are 1 in 4096.

When a pokémon spawns near you an invisible wheel spins and if the needle lands on the shiny spot, the pokémon is shiny. To increase your odds, rather than removing the number of non-shiny spots on the wheel, the wheel stays at 4096 odds but respins, based on any boosts you have.

So for example, if you have a level 3 shiny sandwhich, it will respin, or reroll, the wheel 3 times giving you 3 additional chances for the spawn to be shiny, taking your odds to 1 in 1024.

 Encounter Power :

Additional Notes on Egg Power:

You can increase the hate rate for eggs even more by having a lead Pokémon in your party with the ability: Flame Body.

Flame Body reduces the steps required to hatch a pokemon by 50%.

Pokémon that can have Flame Body in Gen 9 are: Larvesta/Volcarona, Fletchinder/Talonflame, Carkol/Coalossal, and Charcadet.

Sandwich Ingredients

There are four different Stores that sell ingredients for sandwiches and they're located all over Paldea. There is one city, luckily, where you can find all of them: Levincia.

If you fly to the northern Pokémon Center your closest stores are Sure Cans and Aquiesta Supermarket. If you fly to the southern Pokémon Center you'll find Artisian Bakery and Deli Cioso.

Now that you know where to find the stores, you'll need to know where to get specific ingredients that can be used to craft the right sandwhich. The following guide will explain where to get all non-cosmetic sandwich ingredients and how to unlock them.

Please note that certain ingredients are only unlocked at these locations after a number of gym badges have been obtained.
